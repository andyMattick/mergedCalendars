Welcome to HS Rotation Planner

This tool helps you plan your classes with rotation days, units, notes, and test days - all saved in your browser.

  • Enter your class names on the left
  • Define units per period (with topics, quizzes, tests)
  • Click any day to add notes or mark test days
  • Use Backup to save your work

Everything works offline.

Units

Units drive automatic pacing on the calendar.

How to use:

  • Enter Unit Name and Length - + Add Unit
  • Click Edit Days to set topics and mark quizzes/tests per day
  • Calendar will show: AP Stats - Unit 1: Exploring Data (Day 4/10)

Special Days

Mark Family Nights, holidays, or custom days as No Test.

Days after holidays are automatically No Test.

Notes & Assessments

Click any day to open notes.

  • Add per-period notes
  • Mark Test Day or No Test Today
  • Quizzes/Tests from units appear as badges

Printing & Backup

Use Print with Filters for clean monthly views.

Copy Test Days copies a list to clipboard.

Always use Backup/Restore to save your data.
